Why

Each morning presents an opportunity. What we choose to wear is an act of creativity and execution — for some, this happens a few times a day.

We noticed that the fashion content on existing platforms is almost always finished product - I wore this, or I did this and (indirectly) here’s what I wore. But when the dress is picked, and you need help with shoes, or the look was too good not to share, Thisfits provides a space for real-time feedback and documentation.
